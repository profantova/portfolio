Hello, my name is Mirka and I’m a Front End Developer.

The story of how I became a developer began some time ago and didn’t have the most typical start. After completing my PhD (in Culture Studies) at the University of Bratislava, I decided to pursue my true passion: creating websites. It all started with WordPress CMS. I did some basic websites for my family, friends or simply for fun. It was a form of relaxation for me. I learned all I could about WP from online tutorials and spent many hours building different websites just to learn and improve.

Soon, I was getting paid projects from my very first clients. I developed and extended my knowledge about themes, plugins and WordPress functionality. The main part of my work was customization of WP themes according to clients’ wishes; fixing unfinished or poorly developed websites; and general website troubleshooting. As a result, I gained extensive experience of selecting the best themes and plugins and making them work for the client. Around this time I also started to learn a lot about web and graphic design. I did some terrific online courses on UI/UX Design, and absorbed knowledge from the principal texts on the subject.

After moving to Edinburgh in October 2017 I founded a small company called NoIdentity (SK) with my wife, also a web developer. Initially, the services we offered were web design, customization, and building websites with Elementor page builder. But that wasn’t enough for me. I wanted to know how to write code and become a true web developer.

Therefore, I took advantage of online courses with Learn2Code (HTML, CSS, jQuery, Bootstrap, Git, Sass and Design for Screens) and, most recently, with Skillshare (JavaScript and web UX/UI design).

My commitment to constantly add to my coding and design skill set means I have made my goal of becoming a professional web developer and web designer possible. My next goal is to work in a digital agency as a Front End Developer.
